Goal:

Scenario to implement:
- Location: a small industrial room / workshop area.
- Machine: industrial compressor or generator (stationary machine, easier than a vehicle).
- The player starts as the machine operator near the equipment.
- Early signs of danger appear: smoke + warning indicator.
- The project must teach that human safety and evacuation have priority over saving equipment.

Required interaction design:
1) Player notices the warning and can interact with an emergency stop.
2) Player must move to a marked safe distance / safe zone.
3) Player must choose the next action from a small decision UI:
   - raise alarm / call for help
   - evacuate
   - try to save equipment / ignore the danger
4) At least one clearly wrong path must lead to failure and consequences.
5) At least one clearly correct path must lead to success.

Recommended training logic:
- Correct path:
  a) detect smoke/warning,
  b) activate emergency stop,
  c) move to safe zone,
  d) raise alarm / call for help,
  e) evacuate through marked exit.
- Wrong path examples:
  - ignore warning,
  - stay too close to the machine,
  - prioritize saving the machine over evacuation.
- The failure result should show that the fire intensifies / danger increases.
- The success result should clearly state that people safety and evacuation were prioritized.
